## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive strategic evaluation of a startup idea using two globally recognized business frameworks: **SWOT Analysis** and **PESTLE Analysis**.

The objective is to assess startup feasibility, identify growth opportunities, evaluate business risks, analyze market attractiveness, and develop actionable strategic recommendations.

The project simulates how startup founders, investors, management consultants, and business analysts evaluate a business idea before market launch.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

A significant percentage of startups fail due to inadequate strategic planning, weak market understanding, and poor risk assessment.

This project addresses these challenges by analyzing:

* Internal business capabilities
* External market conditions
* Competitive landscape
* Industry opportunities
* Strategic risks
* Long-term growth potential

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

* Evaluate startup feasibility and viability
* Perform SWOT Analysis

* Analyze competitive intensity

## 🛠️ Frameworks Used

### SWOT Analysis

SWOT is a strategic planning framework used to evaluate internal and external business factors.

#### Strengths

* Internal competitive advantages
* Core capabilities
* Unique value propositions

#### Weaknesses

* Resource limitations
* Operational challenges
* Business constraints

#### Opportunities

* Emerging markets
* Technology adoption
* Industry growth

#### Threats

* Competition
* Regulatory changes
* Market disruptions

---

### PESTLE Analysis

PESTLE helps assess macro-environmental factors impacting business performance.

| Factor        | Description                               |
| ------------- | ----------------------------------------- |
| Political     | Government policies and regulations       |
| Economic      | Market conditions and economic trends     |
| Social        | Consumer behavior and demographic changes |
| Technological | Innovation and technology adoption        |
| Legal         | Compliance and regulatory requirements    |
| Environmental | Sustainability and environmental impacts  |

---

## 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The project evaluates startups using strategic business metrics:

| KPI                             | Purpose                                   |
| ------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------- |
| Startup Viability Score         | Overall business feasibility              |
| Growth Potential Score          | Future scalability potential              |
| Market Opportunity Score        | Market attractiveness                     |
| Competitive Intensity Score     | Competitive pressure                      |
| Risk Level Score                | Business risk exposure                    |
| PESTLE Impact Score             | External environmental influence          |
| Innovation Score                | Technology and differentiation capability |
| Investment Attractiveness Score | Investor appeal                           |
